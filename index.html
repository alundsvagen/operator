Frequently asked questions

What is Operator Network?

A closed membership community for Stockholm's C-suite executives — CEOs, CTOs, CFOs, CMOs and founders — focused on building visibility through thought leadership, speaking opportunities and media presence.

Who is it for?

Founders and C-suite executives who want to grow their professional visibility and industry influence, rather than relying on cold outreach or paid advertising alone.

Why does executive visibility matter?

Independent research shows most decision-makers trust thought leadership more than traditional marketing, and a large majority become more receptive to outreach from leaders who consistently publish high-quality insights. See sources above.

How do I apply?

Operator Network is a curated, closed community, so applications are reviewed. Visit the Apply page to submit your details.
