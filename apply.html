Frequently asked questions.

How much does membership cost?

Membership is 850 kr a year. Your first three months are a trial — you can cancel anytime, and your first invoice is only sent after three months, with a reminder email just before.

Who qualifies?

Applicants with at least 2 years of professional experience, in a CEO, CTO, CFO or CMO role, a manager position, or a technical expertise position, working in tech — AI, financial technology or SaaS — and living in Stockholm.

Do I need to be C-suite to apply?

No. The room is built for current and future leaders — manager positions and technical expertise positions qualify alongside CEOs, CTOs, CFOs and CMOs.

Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Your first invoice is sent only after your first three months as a member, and we send a reminder email just before it goes out — so nothing arrives as a surprise.

How do I apply?

Fill out the application form — it takes a few minutes. We review every application for experience, role, sector and fit, and if it's a match we reach out to book a meeting.

I don't live in Stockholm — can I still join?

Not yet. Everything we do happens in person in Stockholm — the dinners, the events, the stages — so membership currently requires living in Stockholm.

Ready to be seen?

Every membership starts with a 30-minute application call. We'll tell you honestly if it's a fit.
